Fish?

Fish are aquatic, vertebrate animals characterized by gills, fins, and scales, typically living in water and breathing through gills. They are a diverse group with over 34,000 known species, ranging from jawless lampreys to bony fishes like tuna and salmon. Fish play a vital role in aquatic ecosystems and are a significant food source for humans.

Glowlight Tetra

Glowlight Tetras are small, peaceful schooling fish known for their glowing red-orange stripe along their sides. They thrive in soft, planted tanks and are best kept in groups of six or more.

Albino Corydoras

Albino Corydoras are peaceful, bottom-dwelling catfish with pale pinkish-white bodies and bright red eyes. They are great for beginners, clean up leftover food, and prefer living in groups.

Harlequin

The Harlequin Rasbora is a colorful, peaceful schooling fish with a reddish-copper body and a distinctive black wedge-shaped marking. Ideal for groups of 8-10 in soft, planted tanks.

Ember Tetra

Ember Tetras are tiny, peaceful fish with a fiery orange color. They thrive in planted aquariums and should be kept in schools of six or more to display their best behavior.
